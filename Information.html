Alpine Club is an after-school club hosted at Kings High School in Seattle, Washington. We will be taking trips (hopefully) up to Stevens pass every week. You can sign up on the website under the Ski Trip Signups page and will receive information via email for the specific trip after you sign up. The price is $25 per trip and the 6th trip will be free for frequent attendors. The cost will be charged directly towards your Kings account and you have to cancel at least before the Wednesday before the trip.
